$$(1) - 1 = 0$$

**Regra de Briott-Ruffini**

$$\lim_{x\to 1}\frac{x^3 -2x^2 + 5x - 4}{x - 1}$$

$$\begin{array}{r|rrr} 1 & 1 & -2 & 5 & -4 \\ \hline & 1 & -1& 4 & 0\\ \end{array}$$
